About This Service

Shooting warmth,
deep recovery

At Santai Recovery Spa, our hot pool is more than just relaxation—it’s the beginning of a complete recovery experience.

The soothing warmth works deep into your muscles, helping to release tension, improve circulation, and calm both body and mind after a long day or intense activity.

Take your time to truly unwind as the heat melts away stress, preparing your body for a deeper level of recovery.

For the best results, we recommend combining your hot pool session with our sauna to enhance detoxification, or challenge your body with an ice bath to boost circulation and speed up recovery.

This contrast of hot and cold creates a powerful reset—leaving you feeling refreshed, re-energized, and fully restored.

Step in, slow down, and experience recovery the Santai way.
